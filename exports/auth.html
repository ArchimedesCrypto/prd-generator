Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Nexus

How secure is my data?

Your data is encrypted at rest and in transit using bank-level 256-bit encryption. We're SOC 2 Type II certified and comply with GDPR, HIPAA, and other major security standards.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. There are no long-term contracts or cancellation fees. Your data will remain accessible for 30 days after cancellation.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full access to all Professional features. No credit card required to start your trial.

What integrations do you support?

We support over 100 integrations including Slack, GitHub, Jira, Figma, Google Drive, Microsoft 365, and many more. We also offer a robust API for custom integrations.

Is there a mobile app?

Yes, we have native iOS and Android apps that work seamlessly with the desktop version. All features are available on mobile, and the app works offline with automatic sync.

How does billing work?

We bill monthly or annually based on the number of active users. You can add or remove users at any time, and we'll prorate the charges accordingly. Annual plans receive a 20% discount.
