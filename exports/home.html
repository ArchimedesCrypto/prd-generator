Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about PRD Generator.

How does the AI generate PRDs?

Our AI is trained on thousands of successful PRDs across different industries. It analyzes your input, understands context, and generates structured documentation following industry best practices. You can then refine and customize the output to match your specific needs.

Can I use my own templates?

Absolutely! While we provide comprehensive templates, you can create and save your own custom templates that match your organization's specific requirements and workflows. Pro and Enterprise plans include unlimited custom templates.

Is my data secure?

Yes. We use enterprise-grade encryption for data in transit and at rest. Your PRDs are private by default, and we never use your data to train our models. Enterprise customers get additional security features including SSO, custom data retention policies, and SOC 2 compliance.

What integrations are available?

We integrate with 50+ tools including Jira, Linear, Notion, Slack, GitHub, Figma, and more. You can sync PRDs, create tasks automatically, and keep your entire team aligned. New integrations are added regularly based on user feedback.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes, there are no long-term contracts. You can cancel your subscription at any time, and you'll continue to have access until the end of your billing period. You can also export all your PRDs before canceling.

Do you offer educational discounts?

Yes! We offer 50% off Pro plans for students, educators, and non-profit organizations. Contact our sales team with proof of eligibility to get started with your discounted account.
